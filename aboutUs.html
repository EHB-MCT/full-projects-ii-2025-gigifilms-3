About us

Who are we?

Gigi Films VZW is a Brussels-based non-profit organization dedicated to creating authentic, socially engaged, and visually powerful film and video productions. From our home base in 1020 Brussels, we develop projects that combine social relevance with artistic expression.

We believe that film is more than just entertainment. It is a medium that can bring people together, strengthen communities, and amplify voices that might otherwise be lost in the noise. Our productions often emerge through collaborations with local creators, artists, social organizations, or community groups. We aim to create space for alternative perspectives, new narratives, and engaged cinema that looks beyond the mainstream.

Our works

Two Minds, One Vision.

Zohra Benhammou & Romy Mana are a filmmakers duo based in Brussels. Their creative partnership began by chance and blossomed into their debut feature documentary, Ana + Yek (Me + You).

As writers and directors, they work beyond genres and formats, exploring (auto)biography, relational dynamics, spaces and places. They seek honest, direct images, always prioritizing relevance and sincerity in their playful, intuitive approach.

Zohra Benhammou (1994, Brussels) is a Belgian-Moroccan filmmaker. Drawing on her background, she delves into the subtleties of personal narratives and heritage. Through her work she aims to convey the nuances of culture, religion and identity, weaving connections that bridge different realms and fostering understanding.

Romy Mana primarily works with performance, sculpture, writing, video and film. In many cases her work combines aspects of different media, blurring boundaries between conventionally distinct categories. She works with a content that can vary from being very personal, a memory, notions of her heritage to the more universal, notions of displacement and loss, finding ways of representing through shifts, ruptures of the visual and linguistic forms of her works.
