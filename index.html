Reframing the Lens of Film

GIGI Films is a Brussels-based production company driven by real people and real stories. We create films and visual projects that are raw, honest, and deeply human. With a strong focus on diversity, street casting, and collaboration, we are committed to building an inclusive film culture—one that gives space to new voices and highlights underrepresented perspectives.

Our Projects

At GIGI Films, we cast real people with real stories. Through street casting and open calls, we look for unique faces and voices that reflect the world around us. No acting experience? No problem. If you have a strong presence or a story to tell, we want to hear from you.


A Portfolio of Meaningful Work

At GIGI Films, we tell stories that matter. Our work spans short films, documentaries, and visual projects rooted in real experiences and honest emotions. Each project is shaped by collaboration, diversity, and a deep respect for the people we portray. From intimate portraits to bold social commentary, we aim to reflect life in all its complexity. Discover our growing body of work—raw, human, and unapologetically real.
